The Spike: How Our Lives Are Being Transformed by Rapidly Advancing Technologies

by Damien Broderick

( Amazon )

Maybe we will live forever after all.

Paul Booth handed me a copy of this book, saying it'd be something I'm interested. We both did the same AI degree , so we've been bought up thinking we're going to live forever in some kind of virtual machine existence at some point. This book seems to be saying that it'll happen some time in the next 15 years, explaining why it'll happen then (Moores law etc).

My rating: Good
