The Salmon of Doubt

by Douglas Adams, Christopher Cerf (Editor)

( Amazon )

Douglas Adams died in 2001, but here's a book from all the stuff they found on his Mac's hard drive.

This is a collection of articles (some short, some long) plus 11 chapters from an unfinished Dirk Gently book. It's all lovely, lovely stuff.

You simply must get a copy.

My rating: Recommended. Unmissable.
