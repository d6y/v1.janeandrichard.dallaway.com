I Am an Oil Tanker

by Fi Glover

( Amazon )

Travel the world and meet quirky local radio.

Yes, it's a travel book. The theme: discover strange, unusual, interesting local radio stations that you're unlikely to know anything about, and visit them and chat with the "talent".

I like radio and listening to local radio whereever I go, so I like this book.

My rating: Good
