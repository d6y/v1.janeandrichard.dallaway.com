All Families Are Psychotic

by Douglas Coupland

( Amazon )

I've got into the habit of liking the way Coupland writes, so much so that I've now lost the ability to say whether this book (this story) is good or not. "I enjoyed it very much", is about the best I can do.

Maybe not quite up to the standards of his previous book, but still great.

My rating: Recommended
