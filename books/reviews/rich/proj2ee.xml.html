Professional Java Server Programming J2EE, 1.3 Edition

by Subrahmanyam Allaramaju, Cedric Buest, Marc Wilcox, Sameer Tyagi, Rod Johnson, Gary Watson, Alan Williamson, John Davies, Ramesh Naggapan, Andy Longshaw, Dr P G Sarang, Tyler Jewell, Alex Toussaint

( Amazon )

Big but packed with useful stuff. Tells you what you need to know.

I've said it before: I hate big books. Especially big techy books. But sometimes you just need that text which you can flip to and be shown the way to do something without having to read lots of prose or spend 20 minutes surfing google. This is one of those kinds of books.

Disclaimer: I know one of the authors.

My rating: Recommended
