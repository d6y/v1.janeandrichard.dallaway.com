Chromosome Six

by Robin Cook

( Amazon )

Action and adventure in New York and West Africa while growing backup body parts inside monkeys.

You see most of the plot changes coming from miles away. The ending is epilog-style and unsatisfying. But I still enjoyed reading it: interesting science ideas, good characters, bad characters, great locations, and a good story right up until the "oh, I'd better end the book now" ending.

My rating: OK
