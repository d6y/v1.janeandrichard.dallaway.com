The Calvin and Hobbes Tenth Anniversary Book

by Bill Watterson

( Amazon )

I've been re-reading all my Calvin and Hobbes books.

Stupendous Boy, Spaceman Spiff, the dinosuars, the snowmen, Tracer Bullet (private eye), Susie, Rosalyn the babysitter...ahhh, such lovely memories. And compared to some cartoons, so well drawn. I'd still have to say Calvin and Hobbes are my fave cartoon of all time.

I've been re-reading all the ones I have: Scientific Progress Goes "Boink", Weirdos from Another Planet, The Revenge of the Baby-Sat, The Lazy Sunday Book, Something Under the Bed is Drooling, The Authoritative Calvin and Hobbes, Attack of the Deranged Mutant Killer Monster Snow Goons, Homicidal Psycho Jungle Cat, The Days Are Just Packed, Yukon Ho!, The Indespensable Calvin and Hobbes, It's a Magical World, There's Treasure Everywhere, and the original Calvin and Hobbes.

My rating: Recommended
