Can You Tell What It Is Yet?

by Rolf Harris

( Amazon - tape | Amazon - book )

Rolf Harris has been on the TV for as long as I can remember, but I didn't know anything about him until I listened to his autobiography.

The tape version is read by him. There are funny bits, sad bits, and I was suprised by how little I knew about where Rolf came from and how he got to be doing what he does. I'm glad I listened to it...and I even wish it had been a bit longer.

My rating: Great
