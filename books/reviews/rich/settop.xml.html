The Essential Guide to Digital Set Top Boxes and Interactive TV

by Gerard O'Driscoll

( Amazon )

No, it's not about which set-top box to buy, it's a book about the technologies, protocols and architectures involved in digital TV.

It's high-level stuff, giving just an overview of the pieces involved (and all the acronyms of) digital TV. Having read the book, I now feel like I know where else to look to get the detailed information I want.

My rating: Good
