The Juicy Guide to the City of Brighton & Hove

by Gilly Smith, Lucy Shuttleworth

( Amazon )

A guide for living in Brighton.

Things to do, places to eat, areas to live in, things for kids. The one or two suggestions for practical advice (painting, electricians) didn't get me anywhere. But we've used the restaurant guide and that seems fair, if brief.

Updates are available at juicyguides.co.uk, via a monthly newsletter if you don't mind Microsoft Word attachments.

My rating: Recommended
