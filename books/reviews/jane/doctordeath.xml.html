Doctor Death

by Jonathan Kellerman

( Amazon )

Another Dr Dellaware thriller from Jonathan Kellerman. I've read quite a few of the Dr Dellaware books and they are always a good read.

This one follows the attempt to solve the murder of a Dr Eldon Mate, a man who for quite some years has been helping people to die. Dr Dellaware is called in to act as a pyschologist to help unravel the mysteries, and finds himself in a conflict with the confidentiality of one of his patients Father, when he becomes a suspect.

Well written, and worth reading even though it is the kind of book that you just want to read another chapter of before going to sleep.

My rating: Good
