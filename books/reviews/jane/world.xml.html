World Snowboard Guide: Where to Snowboard

by Tony Brown

( Amazon )

After a great snowboarding trip we needed help to plan the next one - this seemed like the perfect answer.

It rates lots of countries and their resorts and grades them whilst also giving commentary about their suitability for freeriding, freestyle and importantly for us, beginners.

The only criticism I can lay on this book, is that the proof reading was poor - there are a lot of spelling and grammar errors which detracts from an otherwise great reference.

My rating: useful
