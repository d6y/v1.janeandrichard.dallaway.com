Asleep

by Banana Yoshimoto

( Amazon )

Three short stories, all revolving around the theme of sleep.

I read these on a journey from Chesterfield to Brighton, and I enjoyed them. They didn't require too much thought, and were entertaining. Not earth shatteringly wonderful, but then I didn't want anything too absorbing that would make me miss my station!

My rating: Good train reading
