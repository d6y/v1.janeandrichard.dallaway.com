MindBend

by Robin Cook

( Amazon )

The second Robin Cook book I've read recently where the ending seems like an after thought.

Set in the familiar scene of a hospital and pharmaceutical company, this book follows the life of Adam and his wife Julie. Adam is at medical school, finding finances a struggle and relying on Julie's dancing career to fund them both. When Julie becomes pregnant, he goes and finds work at a large pharmaceutical company and discovers strange goings on during the Doctors conferences that the company sponsors. Goings on which may affect the health of his wife and his unborn child.

My rating: Good
