The Brethren

by John Grisham

( Amazon )

I've read quite a few Grisham books, and this one was dissapointing. It just wasn't as gripping as I normally find them.

The story is perfectly fine, maybe the characters weren't as lifelike as usual. I can't quite put my finger on it.

And despite it being a new book, and me being a well behaved reader - i.e. one that doesn't bend the cover back - several pages fell out before I'd finished it.

My rating: not really worth the money
