Chromosome 6

by Robin Cook

( Amazon )

A typical Robin Cook medical thriller - another real page turner. This is, I think, the first Robin Cook I've read which is about characters I've met in a previous book - Contagion.

I'm not an expert on medical matters - I suspect if I was then I wouldn't enjoy these books so much - but the manner in which medical matters are explained makes it easy to follow.

In this book the plot alternates between Equitorial Guinea and New York and all stems from the killing of a crime lord. What happens next is a few lucky breaks and some not so lucky ones...

My rating: Highly recommended
