The Secrets of Consulting: A Guide to Giving and Getting Advice Successfully

by Gerald M. Weinberg

( Amazon )

Unlike Richard, I'm not very good at reading "work related" books. I often find them too dry and academic. Not this one though.

There are useful chapters, on things from pricing through communication and other tools of the trade. And they're all well written, and a good amount of humour is used to lighten the load a little.

My rating: Very readable
