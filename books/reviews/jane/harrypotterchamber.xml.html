Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

by J K Rowling

( Amazon (book) | Amazon (audio) )

"Harry Potter is a wizard. He is in his second year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Little does he know that this year will be just as eventful as the last..."

This book is as good at the first, and possibly a bit scarier in parts. Again, the tapes are very good too, and Stephen Fry makes a perfect narrator.

Again, the start of the book finds us with the dreary Dursleys, but again, as soon as we leave there and go back into the magical world of wizardry things become far more entertaining.

My rating: Good
