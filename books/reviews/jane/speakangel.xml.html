Speaking with the Angel

by Compiled by Nick Hornby

( Amazon )

A collection of short stories by some very well known authors - Nick Hornby, Roddie Doyle compiled in this offering to raise money and awareness of The Treehouse Trust, a charity for autistic children.

Easy reading, some of these stories are excellent, some are a bit odd. But all in all it's not a "buy this book because it's for charity" but a "buy this book because it's good, oh and by the way some money goes to the Treehouse Trust as well".

My rating: Buy it!
