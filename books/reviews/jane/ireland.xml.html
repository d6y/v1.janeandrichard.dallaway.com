Around Ireland with a Fridge

by Tony Hawks

( Amazon )

Tony Hawks (an English comedian) is bet 100 GBP that he can't hitch-hike around Ireland with a fridge.

This book is his account of how he got on, and how the fridge probably became more famous than him.

Having written this review, I've discovered that my copy of this book is missing. If you've seen it, please send it back!

My rating: Excellent
