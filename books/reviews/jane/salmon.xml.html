The Salmon of Doubt

by Douglas Adams, Christopher Cerf (Editor)

( Amazon )

This is a collection of Douglas's articles from magazines, interviews as well as the unfinished Dirk Gently novel "Salmon of Doubt".

For me, the unfinished novel was of the least importance. The articles were really intelligently written, and I'm embarassed to admit that I had never realised quite how clever Douglas was until I read this.

Read it.

My rating: Recommended.
