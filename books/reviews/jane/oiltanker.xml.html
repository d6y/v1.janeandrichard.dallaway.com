I Am an Oil Tanker

by Fi Glover

( Amazon )

So, Fi Glover decided to go and visit some local radio stations around the world to find out their stories.

It seemed to have a lot of potential, but just didn't quite deliver. It tries to be really light hearted, but doesn't quite achieve it.

However, I am glad I read it, if for no other reason than finding out about the radio station in montserrat who stayed on air whilst the volcano on that island was erupting, and became literally a lifeline to the islands residents.

My rating: Interesting in parts
