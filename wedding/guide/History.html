History

How we met

In 1994 Jane was working for Speedwing Logica whilst Richard was working for Logica Cambridge. We met on a graduate training course held in London. As was usual, the Speedwing graduates had arranged to meet up with other friends in one of the local pubs, and Richard and some of the other Cambridge people came too. During the course of the evening, we chatted a lot, and Richard passed Jane one of his business cards so that she could get in touch. He also left one with Karen in case Jane lost hers. At the end of the evening Jane trotted off with Heather and Jason to head off to Ealing, and Richard headed back to Cambridge. During the following week Richard sent Jane a postcard, and Jane (egged on by Karen) invited Richard to join them for Jane's birthday drinks in the Punch and Judy pub in Covent Garden. He accepted and we got on well.

What happened next

Between 1994 and 1997 we both lived in shared places, and were starting to get a bit fed up of it, so in 1997 we bought the flat in Ealing. We moved in and passed a happy 3 years before deciding to move on. We decided to move to Brighton as we wanted to have more opportunities to spend time outdoors. We moved here last August and have enjoyed our first year here.

Engagement

On February 9th 2002 Richard proposed to Jane. She accepted and from then on lots and lots of wedding planning has been taking place
