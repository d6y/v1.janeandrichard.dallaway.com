Where & When

The wedding will take place on Saturday 7th September 2002 at 4pm. The service will be held at St Nicholas Church, Hull. After the service there will be a reception at the Londesborough Arms in Market Weighton.

To make it easier for you to get around, a private coach has been laid on to pick up guests from the Londesborough, and a few other local hotels, and take you to St Nicholas Church, and then to return you to the Londesborough after the service. You'll need to let us know if you want to make use of the coach (see What You Need To Do).
