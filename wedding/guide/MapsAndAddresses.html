Maps and Addresses

St Nicholas Church

Hessle High Road
Hull
HU4 6SA

Map 1: From the exit off the M18 to the church:


Directions:

  • Take the exit marked M62 Hull at the end of the M18
  • Drive along the M62 until it ends and becomes the A63
  • Drive along the A63 for approximately 12 miles, driving under the Humber bridge.
  • Exit the A63 at the junction towards Hessle (this is the first exit after passing under the Humber Bridge). Then follow map 2.


Map 2: From the exit of the A63 to the church:



Directions:

  • Exit the A63 at the junction towards Hessle (this is the first exit after passing under the Humber Bridge).
  • At the roundabout at the top of the slip road turn left.
  • Continue straight on past Sainsburys towards another roundabout
  • Turn right onto Hessle Road
  • Travel about half a mile and turn left into Pickering Road.
  • The church is on the corner of Pickering Road and Hessle Road, and parking is either in the Church car park or anyway on the roads.

Map 3: From the church to the Londesborough Arms hotel:



Directions:

  • Leave the church and join Hessle Road. (See Map 2 for more help)

  • At the first roundabout, take the FIRST exit
  • At the second roundabout, take the FIRST exit
  • Don't take the side turning, instead continue towards the main road out of Hull (A63)
  • Join the A63.
  • Continue on the A63 for 8.2 miles, and turn left towards A1034 / South Cave / MKT. Weighton / York
  • Bear left (West) onto A1034 for 7.6 miles
  • At roundabout, take the SECOND exit for 65 yds
  • Exit roundabout onto Sancton Road for 0.2 mi
  • Continue on Southgate for 0.2 mi
  • Turn left onto Beverley Road for 43 yds
  • Bear right onto High Street
  • The Londesborough Arms is on your right. There is a car park through the archway.
