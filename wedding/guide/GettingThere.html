Getting There

Hull is located in the north east of England, approximately 3 hours from London by train and 4 or more hours by car.

By Rail:

Hull is served by GNER and Hull Trains operating out of Kings Cross station. If you want to come direct to the wedding from the train, then get off the train in Hull and get a taxi from the station to the church. To get to the Londesborough or most of the other hotels we've suggested, get off the train at Brough.

By Car:

Hull is at the east end of the M62 and can be reached by either the M1 or the A1. Leaving either motorway join the M18 and then the M62 following signs for Hull. See Maps and Addresses for a couple of maps of the area.
