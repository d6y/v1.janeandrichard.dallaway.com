Gift Ideas

Please do not feel obliged to buy us a gift, it'll be great just to have you with us. If, however, you want to buy something to help us to remember our special day we have a list registered at Debenhams.

Our wedding number with Debenhams is 855291.

Gifts can be purchased from your nearest Debenhams store – if you head towards the Wedding Service Department you should find a touch screen which you can type our number into to get the list. You can then walk around the store with the list and choose a gift that you'd like to buy. When you pay for your gift, please remember to tell them that the gift is from our wedding list.

They can also be bought by telephone – simply call 0870 606 4242. The opening times are:

Monday to Friday 8am – 10pm
Saturday 9am – 8pm
Sunday 11am – 4pm

They can also be bought online at http://www.debenhams.com. Select Weddings and then “Browse and buy from a wedding list”.

Buying online or by telephone will incur a delivery charge of £3.50. This is per order, not per item.

There is also a gift wrapping service which costs an additional £3, again per order.

We also have a few other ideas which can be found at http://www.janeandrichard.co.uk/wedding/gifts.html
