Food & Drink

You'll be met at the Londesborough Arms with drinks, and we're laying on wine throughout the meal with soft drinks for the children. There will be a cash bar available if you want something different.

The menu choices are:

Starter:

Homemade Tomato and Basil soup
Prawn Salad with Marie Rose dressing
Honeydew Melon with Fresh Fruit and Blackcurrant sorbet

Main course:

Roast Chicken Breast with a Madeira and Mushroom sauce
Salmon Fillet in a Basil Butter sauce
Mediterranean vegetables en Croute with Tomato Provencale
Children's choice – Chicken Nuggets, Chips and Beans

Dessert:

Fresh Fruit Pavlova with Red Fruit Coulis
Chocolate Fudge Gateaux
Warm Apple Slice with vanilla ice cream
Children's choice – Ice cream


You'll need to select one from each course (or just a Main course and Dessert for the children) and let us know. See the What you need to do section for details on how to get that information to us.
