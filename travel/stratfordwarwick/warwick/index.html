Warwick castle

Having explored Stratford on Saturday we headed off to Warwick castle. Parking cost us £2, and then it was £11.25 each to get in. We thought this made a quite expensive day out.

However, there is a lot to see and do at Warwick Castle, there are some parts which are more like a stately home, and other bits which are full of waxwork exhibitions. The castle is very well preserved and the displays are very interesting, and very well done.
