New Year in Meribel

A 4.5 day snowboarding trip to Les 3 Vallees over New Year. A great trip, with some wonderful snow on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day. We spent New Years Eve (and in fact most other evenings) at La Tsaretta in the village.

A great break, and Meribel and the surrounding area is definitely a resort I could return to.
