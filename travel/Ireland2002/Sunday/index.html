Galway to Westport

Our first days cycle. We were taken by coach from the Brook Lodge B&B in Galway to Cong, and then cycled to Westport. It was a route of approximately 39 miles, and at times it was hard going. The weather was wonderful and a lot of us ended the day with sunburnt noses. (21 images.)
