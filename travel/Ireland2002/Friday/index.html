Carreroe to Galway

We had an early start to catch the ferry from Rossaveel to Inishmor (the largest of the Aran islands). We spent most of the day on the island, and then caught the ferry back and said Goodbye to our bikes and got a minibus back into Galway in preparation for our final night dinner. (20 images.)
