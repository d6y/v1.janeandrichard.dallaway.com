Snorkeling

We got into snorkeling whilst in the Maldives and found very few references on the web for snorkel rather than dive.

To try and combat this, I started a mailing list on egroups.com (now yahoogroups.com) for snorkeling discussions. It is fairly quiet a lot of the time, but there are usually some great responses to questions.
