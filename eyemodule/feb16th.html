A day in the life of me
February 16th 2001


I left the house


and got on a tube from our local station.


I got off the tube at Chancery Lane and walked to the office.


I sat at my desk at Aspect and did some work before


taking a black cab to a client site.


I spent the rest of my day there - these are the plants in their reception area.



At around 6pm, I left the client site and walked past the horses fountain on Haymarket


and past the advertising at Picadilly Circus before getting a tube back home.


We had a lovely bottle of red wine, and a pasta dinner.
