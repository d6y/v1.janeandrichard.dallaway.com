Hyde Park in the Snow
28 December 2000

As it had snowed in London for the first time in ages, I met up with Kev,a friend of ours, and we went for a walk in the snow. Kev is a South African and so had only ever been in snow once before. So, we made a snowman, a fairly small snowman standing about 2 foot tall.
