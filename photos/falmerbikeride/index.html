Falmer bike ride

Well, this week we did the CTC Trails by Rail route from Falmer station. This was a really good fun ride, although the first part was really, really hard work - lots of mud and deep ruts which were hard to cycle through. When we joined the South Downs Way it got much better. (12 images.)
