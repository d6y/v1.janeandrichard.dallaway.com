A weekend in Margate

We spent the weekend in Margate with both sets of parents. On Sunday we went for a walk along the cliff top to the Captain Digby, past a ruined building which seems to be part of a golf course and back again. This meant I get to get some cuddles from young Millie, as well as spending some time with Reece and Jack.
