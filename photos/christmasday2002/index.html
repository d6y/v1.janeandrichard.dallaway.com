Christmas Day 2002

Our day started with a wander to watch the Brighton Swimming Club and friends do the traditional Christmas Day swim.

Then, back home to drink Cava, open presents (a new camera for Jane), play with presents, cook Christmas Dinner, eat it, and then to relax... A good day
