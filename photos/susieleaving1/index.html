Susie leaving

Susie is leaving Northern-Atlas, Brighton and the UK next weekend, so we decided to celebrate this we'd have an evening out. We started off at our place for drinkies before moving on to The Gingerman. We had a lovely meal there before heading over to Susie's place to drop some stuff off. Our final stop was at the Grand Hotel for cocktails. A very enjoyable evening, and a good send off for Suz. (16 images.)
