A lunchtime walk with Freddie

We went with Jeremy and Freddie on a lunchtime walk from Devil's Dyke down to the Shepherd and Dog pub in Fulking and back.

Jeremy and I realised how pleased we were that we didn't attempt this on our bikes when we did our bike ride a few weeks ago - we'd never have made it!
