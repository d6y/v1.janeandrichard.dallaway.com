Susie leaving drinks

To celebrate Susie leaving the UK, we went and played pub golf. This involves drinking in 9 pubs, and drinking the specified quantity to make par. We started in the Grand Hotel with cocktails, then on to the Walkabout Lodge, the Fiddlers Elbow, the Cricketers, the Bath Arms (the only pub I don't have a photo of us all outside of), the font and firkin, the Victory and ending in the Western Front. We ran out of time so we didn't make it to either the 9th hole or the Club House. During the evening we presented Susie with some UK tourist tack. (28 images.)
