Jane's folks visit

My folks came down for a visit and so we took some time out to explore around the area a bit more. We spent one day wandering around Brighton, getting them aclimatised to the area. We spent one day exploring Devil's Dyke, Bramber castle, Poyning and Steyning. The one rainy day of their visit we headed off to the Brighton Pavilion which was a very interesting way to spend a few hours. Then on their final day we spent some time at the Marina, before driving off along the coast through Newhaven and Seaford and then on past the Long Man of Wilmington before stopping for a wander around Lewes. All in all, a good excuse for exploring. (17 images.)
