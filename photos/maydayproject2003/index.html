Mayday project 2003

The idea was to take a series of photos during the day for the Mayday Project. We had visitors, and so after some light shopping we went to Drusillas Park where the middle section of the photos were taken. I forgot to take a photo of any of our meals, but did remember to take a photo of the Monopoly board just before we finished packing it up.
