Dunstable Kite Festival

We headed off to the Dunstable Kite Festival (29 July 2001) and had a fun day. It was very hot, and the breeze was stronger, and more regular than at the Newbury festival a few weeks ago. The guy doing the announcing was very good, and explained what was happening in every demonstration/event, and gave us a real insight into why certain things were happening. For instance, during the buggying event he explained what size kite was being used, what buggy, why he was wearing a harness - this was really informative. All in all, we had a good day out.
