Sand Sculptures 2006

The family came down to Brighton at the weekend to visit the Sand Sculpture Festival at the Marina. As last year, some pretty impressive sculptures. This year more of them were undercover, making the photography rather more challenging - still, it started to pour with rain whilst we were looking at them so it worked out well for us.
