A short bike ride in April

We collected Susie and took her out for a gentle Sunday cycle. We cycled down Marine Parade, and discovered the Morris Minor Car Rally before heading off through the Marina, to Rottingdean and back over the clifftops into Brighton. A gentle but pleasant cycle. (5 images.)
