Brands Hatch

We bought Paul a driving experience at Brands Hatch to say thank you for everything he put in to our wedding day.

The idea was that he'd be driven around the circuit at terrifying speeds by someone who knew what they were doing. The organization wasn't great, in that we were told to arrive around 5:30pm, but were then informed "yeah, we don't know why they give those times out", because Paul didn't get out onto the track until about 7:45pm. Still, it gave us plenty of time to see what else was going on. Overall, as a day out it's pretty good. Paul rated the experience and wanted to know how to drive like a professional. So, maybe we'll be back.

Tip: if you're thinking of doing something like this, book directly with Brands Hatch, and you'll probably save yourself some money.
