Sand Sculpture Festival

We wandered down to the Sand Scupture Festival in Brighton at the weekend. Some really impressive sculptures, and a great idea of what the real structures in Egypt must be like.

Slightly amused by the fact that there was a hot dog/burger van, and a bar area within the complex - not sure how Egyptian that is!
