Pioneer Bike Run

I saw it mentioned in a listings magazine and thought it might be worth a look. I hadn't realised that the Pioneer Bike Run was going to be quite so popular, and have quite so many bikes. It was a beautiful sunny day, and Brighton was full of old bikes as well as new bikes. We headed down and wandered around for an hour or so before taking refuge on the beach. (18 images.)
