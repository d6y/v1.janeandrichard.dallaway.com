Brighton Festival 2003

The Brighton Festival is over for another year and these are a selection of the photos we've taken over the past month.

This year we decided to make the most of the Festival and attended several events (some free, others paid for). Next year I hope we'll manage to do the same again. The events that this album cover are:

  • The Childrens Parade
  • Don't Look Back
  • Constant Soldier
  • Sister Sister
  • Day of Dance
  • Samba Carnival Parade
  • The Depot
