Save our seafront protest

Today there was a demonstration against a large development that is part of the renovation of the West Pier. It's a picket across the entrance of a hotel where I believe the plans were being discussed by the developers. It's a bit cold and windy, but there were enough people to make an impression, and cars where hooting their approval as they went past. I'm told that these plans and alternatives will be on display to the public in a few weeks. More information over at the Save our seafront site (they don't like the plan) and also at the West Pier Trust site (who do like the plan, I think). (6 images.)
