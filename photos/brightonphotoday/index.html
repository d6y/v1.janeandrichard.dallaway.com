Photo Day

Seeing as how both Joe and I were unemployed at this point of December, we decided it would be a good time to have a photo day down here in Brighton. We'd forgotten quite how cold it would be, and neither of us took quite as many photos as we otherwise might have as it was just too tempting to keep our hands in our pockets. Joe will put his photos up on his site at some point (or so he keeps telling me). (8 images.)
