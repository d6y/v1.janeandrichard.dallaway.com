Ice Skating at Kew

Jeremy decided to treat Kirsty to a surprise trip ice skating at an outdoor rink. Initially the plan was to go to Somerset house, but it was almost completely booked up and so he decided on Kew Gardens instead. He invited us to go with them, and so we met them there and had a good time skating around before deciding to escape off the ice for some lovely hot chocolate.
