Snowboarding and Chesterfield

We booked in at the Snowdome near Birmingham for our first snowboarding lessons. There's a report on our snowboarding over at igloolife.com.

While we were in the area we spent the weekend at Jeremy and Kirsty's place in Chesterfield, on the edge of the Peak District and had a good walk around the countryside.
