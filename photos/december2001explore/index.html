December exploring

Despite it being very cold, the sun was shining and we decided to head out on a bit of an explore of the coast towards and beyond Worthing. We had beach stops at Widewater Lagoon, Pagham and Selsey, and a lunch stop at the Marine Gardens Restaurant in Worthing. (10 images.)
