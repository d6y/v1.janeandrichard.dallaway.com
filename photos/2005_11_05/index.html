Lewes Bonfire 2005

Another trip to Lewes for Bonfire, although it was really busy this year - probably something to do with it being 400 years and also a Saturday.

We were a group of 9 this year, 7 had never been before - and I think everyone enjoyed the experience. Due to an unfortunate fainting incident we didn't get to hang around long enough for bonfire or fireworks but we did get to see a lot of torches, and some of the party got to see the inside of the Ambulance Station.
