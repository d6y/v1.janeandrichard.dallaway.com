Brighton Festival Big Weekend

On Saturday we took a wander along the North Laines to watch some of the street performers. We then wandered down to the sea front in search of food, and found a few more performers performing. In the evening we went to see the fireworks in Preston Park which were fantastic. This was all part of the Brighton Festival. (17 images.)
