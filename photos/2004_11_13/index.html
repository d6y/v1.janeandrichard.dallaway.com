The Lakes

To celebrate Mum's 70th birthday we all went to visit family friends in the Lake District. We spent most of our time in the market town of Broughton In Furness, but we took advantage of the beautiful weather on the Saturday and went on a drive along Wrynose Pass and into Ambleside.
