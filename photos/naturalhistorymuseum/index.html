Natural History Museum

We met up with friends at the Natural History Museum, probably the London museum we've spent the most time of over the years. Chris (aged 6) wanted to see the dinosaurs, as is only right and proper for a little boy, and so we headed there first. Both him and his older sister, Caty, were entertained by the vast array of interactive exhibitions - buttons, levers, etc. I have to say that I couldn't get over the beauty of the building itself.
