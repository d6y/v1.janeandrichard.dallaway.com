London

Our first day trip to London for a while. The main reason was to meet up with my parents, but their train didn't get in till 11am, giving us some time to visit the Apple Store. Total purchase: 1 iPod Shuffle, 1 USB iPod charger, 1 mouse. Mostly what we'd planned. I was pretty impressed with the iPod Mini (much smaller than I'd imagined) and the iPod Photo (very clear screen). Richard was mostly impressed with the 30 inch cinema display.

After meeting up with Mum and Dad we spent the afternoon at the British Museum mostly concentrating on the Egyptian collection. We had dinner at Browns before they headed off home after a lovely day.
