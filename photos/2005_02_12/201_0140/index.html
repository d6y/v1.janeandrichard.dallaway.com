Apple Store, Regent Street




We visited the Apple Store in New York when we were there in September, but had failed to come up with a good enough reason to visit since the London store's opening in November. We were there just after 10am, and in the half an hour we were there it started to get pretty busy. A nice, airy store, with beautiful products to lust after.
