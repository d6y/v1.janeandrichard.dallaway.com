Snow on the downs

It had snowed in Sussex, and this seemed like another good opportunity to take the camera out to play. So, we headed up to Devil's Dyke and went for a very brief walk (it really was pretty damn cold) before my fingers stopped being able to press the shutter and we got back into the car to defrost.
