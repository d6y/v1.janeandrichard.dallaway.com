SoundsGood Energy Clip

An excellent add on to the mp3 player as it means you don't need to have the player plugged into the visor to listen to it.

It's very simple - one AA battery, a slot for the module to sit in, and a belt clip - that's it. I like this as it means I'm not as restricted as to when I can listen to music.

What you don't get is any LCD type display, and you can't swap between without losing your place amongst the tracks. But other than that - no complaints.

So far the battery life seems pretty good too. Over 8 hours from one AA battery.
