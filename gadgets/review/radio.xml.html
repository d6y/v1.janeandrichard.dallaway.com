Sony Walkman Radio SRF-M95

Another thing that was in my bag when it was stolen was the Sounds Good MP3 player. So, I needed something new to use to keep me amused on the way to work. This time I decided to buy a radio.

I started off looking at the RDS type radios - I thought the idea of a radio that tuned itself as I travelled was really good. These turned out to be quite large and so I decided against this. Instead I started looking at the small radios with digital tuning. I decided on the Sony because it had both FM and AM and was nice and small.

So far I'm pleased with my choice. I use it most days for my journey to and from work, and apart from a few blackspots (mostly due to the train going through tunnels) the reception is good.
